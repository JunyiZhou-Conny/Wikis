# China–US tech investment barriers

Investment access to frontier tech (AI, space, quantum) is narrowing from **both** jurisdictions.

## US side

- **SpaceX IPO (2026):** Underwriters rejected orders from **mainland China & Hong Kong** (ITAR).
- **Investment screening expansion** → passive/financial stakes in sensitive tech.

## China side

- **Cross-border broker crackdown** — Futu, Tiger, Longbridge.
- **Capital account:** Personal FX not for overseas **securities** investment.

## Asymmetry

- **China → US (for investment):** Tightly restricted.
- **US → China (repatriating legal USD):** Channels exist but subject to **SAFE quotas**, customs, and **tax reporting**.
