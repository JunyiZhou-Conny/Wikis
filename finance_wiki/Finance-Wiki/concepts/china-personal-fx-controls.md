# China personal FX controls

Mainland individuals face **SAFE-administered** rules on buying, holding, and moving foreign currency.

## Annual facilitation quota

- **USD 50,000 equivalent / person / calendar year** for purchase **and** settlement.
- Does **not** roll over year to year.

## Prohibited uses (even within quota)

- Overseas **securities** investment
- Overseas **real estate**
- **Investment-linked** insurance
- Other **capital account** items not yet opened

## Legitimate large outflows (can exceed $50k)

| Purpose | Typical documents |
|---------|-------------------|
| **Study abroad tuition** | Admission letter, school invoice |
| **Living expenses for student** | Passport, enrollment, expense proof |
| **Medical abroad** | Hospital bills |

## Cash physical export (foreign currency)

| Amount (USD equiv.) | Rule |
|---------------------|------|
| ≤ 5,000 | Generally no permit |
| 5,000 – 10,000 | Bank **携带外汇出境许可证** |
| > 10,000 | Generally prohibited (exceptions need SAFE) |

**RMB cash** outbound: typically ≤ **CNY 20,000**.
