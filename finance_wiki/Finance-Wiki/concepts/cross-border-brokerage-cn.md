# Cross-border brokerage (China context)

**跨境券商** are brokerages licensed **outside mainland China** that marketed **securities services to mainland residents** without a **CSRC** mainland brokerage license.

## Who they serve

| Client | Typical need |
|--------|--------------|
| **Mainland residents** (core) | Access US/HK stocks |
| **US residents with SSN** | **Do not need** these—use Schwab, Fidelity, IBKR US |

## 2026 enforcement

- **May 2026:** CSRC proposed heavy fines—Futu ~CNY 1.85B, Tiger ~CNY 411M.
- **2026–2028:** Mainland accounts **sell & withdraw only**—no new deposits or buys.

## Direct vs indirect access

| Channel | Mechanism |
|---------|-----------|
| **Direct** | US person + US broker → real equity |
| **Indirect (broker)** | Mainland client → Futu/Tiger → offshore order |
| **Indirect (proxy)** | A-share thematic stocks, ETFs, pre-IPO perps |
