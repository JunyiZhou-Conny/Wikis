# Bubble lifecycle stages

A practical map of how asset bubbles form, inflate, and deflate—combining narrative, liquidity, fundamentals, and greater-fool dynamics.

## Formation prerequisites

1. **Grand narrative** + **ample credit/liquidity**
2. **Financial innovation** and/or **regulatory easing**
3. **Productivity story** → revenue/earnings growth → hiring & capex boom

## Seven stages

| Stage | Name | What happens |
|-------|------|--------------|
| 1 | Fundamental bid | Real value creation; DCF still relevant |
| 2 | Greater fool | Price driven by resale, not discounted cash flows |
| 3 | Financial engineering peak | High-valuation IPOs, SPOs, stock M&A |
| 4 | Liquidity turn | New money slows; leverage maxed; dispersion rises |
| 5 | Disappointment & break | Growth misses; forced de-leveraging |
| 6 | Dead-cat bounces | Sharp, short rallies; last-resort buyers |
| 7 | Mean reversion | Vol falls; prices drift toward fundamentals |

## Application to AI / SpaceX (2026)

- Stage 3 signals: SpaceX record IPO, OpenAI IPO expected.
- Stage 4 pressure: Doubling SpaceX from here requires enormous incremental capital.
- Policy: Verbal hawkishness may **delay** stage 5 without removing it.
