# SpaceX IPO 2026 (SPCX)

Space Exploration Technologies Corp. listed on Nasdaq as **SPCX** on **2026-06-12**.

## Deal facts

| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| Ticker | SPCX |
| Offer price | $135 / share (fixed) |
| Primary shares | ~555.6M (+ ~83.3M overallotment) |
| Gross proceeds | ~$75–86B to company |
| Implied market cap | ~$1.77T |
| Structure | 100% primary |

## Valuation vs fundamentals

- Market cap ranks among largest US companies while **revenue and profit** remain far below other trillion-dollar peers.
- Investment case is heavily **Musk + optionality** (Starlink, Starship, space datacenter/AI narrative).

## Trading after IPO

- IPO primary block sold once at offering; **does not cap** future trading.
- Retail typically buys **after listing** from other investors; see [[ipo-proceeds-vs-market-cap]].
