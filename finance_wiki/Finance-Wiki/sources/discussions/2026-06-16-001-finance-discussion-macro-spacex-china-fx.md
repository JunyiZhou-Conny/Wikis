## Summary

Multi-thread discussion covering:

1. **Fed tightening & crisis history** — Post-1980s pattern: Fed funds above ~5% often precedes regional crises. Current cycle: hikes to 5.25–5.5% (2023), first cut 50bp (2024-09-19). 2. **China exposure** — Cannot decouple from global markets; cross-border brokers (Futu, Tiger, Longbridge) penalized; mainland clients restricted to sell-only during 2026–2028 transition.

5. **Cross-border brokerage** — Offshore brokers licensed outside mainland but not CSRC-licensed for mainland securities business.
